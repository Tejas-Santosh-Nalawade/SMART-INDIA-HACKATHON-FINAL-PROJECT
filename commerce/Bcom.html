Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)

A 3-year undergraduate program focusing on business management, marketing, finance, and human resources.

Career Opportunities:

Business Consultant, Marketing Executive, HR Manager

A globally recognized 2-year program focusing on advanced business management, marketing, finance, and operations.
